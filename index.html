History summary

Luigi gets an invitation, stating that he won an entire mansion for himself! Excited, he tells this to his brother, who without hesitation, runs at full speed towards the address, Luigi follows him, but upon arriving, he realizes that things could not be as he expected.

Bosses

Chauncey

This little baby is the first boss of the game, but don't let him fool you!
This little guy can give you a headache if you don't know how to fight him!
To defeat him simply throw back the balls he throws at you, and then he will be vulnerable.

Bogmire

Bogmire can be found in the cementary section of the game, he attacks by using his shadow, so be carefull!
To defeat him you'll have to use the shadows that he use to attack you,
create a ball with them and throw the ball back at him, and then he will be vulnerable.

Boolossus

This boss is one of the hardest bosses in the game.
This GIANT boo is a combination of lots of boos, that join their forces in a try to attack Luigi!
To defeat him, you'll have to get him closer to one of the unicorn statues surronding the area, and when he pops,
use the ice power to freeze all the boos and then capture them.
This, itself, is not so hard, but the problem comes when there is only ONE boo, because this last one will be very hard to catch.

King Boo

The king of all boos finally face Luigi! And he is not alone...
King Boo will use a Bowser custome to fight Luigi, using all the attacks of the original Bowser!
In order to defeat King Boo, you'll have to wait until he throw spike balls at you, and then throw them back at him!
By doing this, the Bowser custome will lose his head, and King Boo will leave the suit, then you can start to capture him.
Just be carefull with Bowser's head... ice breath...?
